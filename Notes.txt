CALIBURN:
1   OnInitializeAsync   First time setup (only runs once).
2   OnActivateAsync     Runs every time the view becomes active.
3   OnViewAttached      The View and ViewModel are paired.
4   OnViewLoaded        The XAML is loaded into memory.
5   OnViewReady         The View is fully visible to the user.
6   OnDeactivateAsync   Runs when the view is hidden or closed.
